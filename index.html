JAMES WYNN

Founder, CEO & Notorious Trader

James Wynn is a notorious high-stakes crypto trader, globally recognized for turning conviction, timing, and calculated aggression into some of the most talked-about trades in crypto history. In just 70 days, Wynn harnessed the power of leverage to transform a relatively small amount of capital into a staggering $87,000,000. During this period James Wynn did the largest ever publicly traded $BTC long of $1.26 billion dollars on the on-chain trading platform HyperLiquid.

But his story began long before that.

James Wynn emerged during the early era of meme coins, immersing himself deeply in internet culture, market psychology, and on-chain trends before most traders even understood the space. Many described him as having a "sixth sense" for spotting winners before the masses caught on.

One of the most notable examples came when Wynn publicly posted on X about $PEPE while it was sitting at roughly a $600,000 market cap. At the time, he stated with complete conviction that the project would reach "multi-billions" in valuation. The call later proved correct, with Wynn cashing out over $25,000,000 from the trade.

He repeated similar plays across multiple meme coins — often identifying low-cap opportunities long before mainstream attention arrived. Over time, this cemented his reputation as one of the few traders with a proven eye for discovering explosive asymmetric opportunities before the market fully recognized them.

Since achieving tremendous success within the crypto industry, Wynn has set his sights on building something far greater — combining years of experience, market knowledge, and sharp business acumen into a full suite of products under one unified umbrella.

At the core of this vision is community. Wynn witnessed firsthand the power of collective belief, culture, and aligned incentives through his deep involvement in community-driven meme coins — and what becomes possible when people move in unity together with conviction. That understanding now serves as the foundation — and secret weapon — to tackle the mainstream exchanges and instead offer people the freedom to deposit and withdraw at their free-will without the need for intermediaries such as banks, KYC, personal information which is prone to hacks, or suspicious suspension of withdrawals in times of volatility.

As the saying goes in crypto: "Not your keys, not your coins" — and the same goes for your trades: "Not your keys, not your trades."

That understanding now serves as the foundation behind the next chapter: creating a community-powered brand designed to accelerate growth, innovation, and long-term impact across multiple sectors of the crypto space.
